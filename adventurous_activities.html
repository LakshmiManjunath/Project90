Boston has different activities for you all through out the year. This is the best city for active and social crowd. The winter offers lot of sking,snow-boarding, ice skating places. And if you dont like to go out, you have football season at its peak during these months that keeps you competetive.

Summer has its own share of endless activites, from hiking, swimming in the umpteen bleaches all around BOSTON, ziplining, sky-diving, para-gliding, the list is just too big.

In general you wont run out of things to do in Boston.
